Buying Guide

What to Look For in a Uv Protection Rash Guard Women'S

Buying a surf rash guard gets easier once you know which details to check first.

Prioritize flat-lock seamsFlat seams reduce chafing against a surfboard or wetsuit during long paddling sessions, unlike raised seams that can rub raw over time.
Check the zip's backingA zip guard flap behind the zipper prevents it from scratching skin or a wetsuit during duck dives and quick direction changes.
Match sleeve length to session lengthShort sleeves suit quick sessions with lower sun exposure, while all-day surf days usually justify the extra coverage of long sleeves.
