Buying Guide

What to Look For in a Patio Umbrella With Lights

A clip-on solar umbrella light is worth it when you already own an umbrella and just want to add usable light after dark.

Confirm the clip fits your pole diameterUmbrella, tent, and gazebo poles vary in thickness, so check the clip's opening range before buying to avoid a loose or overly tight fit.
Check the waterproof rating against your use caseAn IP65 rating handles rain and splashes fine, but if it'll sit fully submerged or exposed to constant hose-down cleaning, look for a higher IP rating.
Decide if you need one lamp or severalA single clip-on light covers a small table well, but larger patio setups usually need two or three spaced around the canopy edge for even coverage.
