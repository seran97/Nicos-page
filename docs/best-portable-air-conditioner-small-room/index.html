  1. Assess Your Room Size & Humidity Level

    Evaporative portable air conditioners like this one work best in dry climates and rooms under 150–200 sq ft. Measure your space and check your local average humidity. If you're in a dry region (under 50% relative humidity), an evaporative cooler delivers excellent results at a fraction of the cost of compressor-based units.
