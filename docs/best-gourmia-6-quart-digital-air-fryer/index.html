Buying Guide

What to Look For in a Gourmia 6-Quart Digital Air Fryer

Choosing between digital air fryer models often comes down to a short list of practical priorities.

Decide if a viewing window is worth it to youSome buyers value being able to glance at food without opening the basket, while others find it an unnecessary feature that just adds a surface to clean. Consider your own cooking habits before paying extra for a window feature.
Match capacity to your typical meal sizeA 6-quart basket suits families or batch cooking, but if you usually cook for one or two people, a smaller unit will preheat faster and take less counter space.
Check preset coverage against your regular mealsLook at whether the presets align with foods you cook often — fries, chicken, vegetables, reheating — rather than assuming more presets automatically means more usefulness.
