Buying Guide: 3 Steps to the Perfect Oversized Beach Towel

Don't overpay or under-buy. Follow these three steps before clicking add to cart.

Check the Size — Bigger Isn't Always Better

The sweet spot for an oversized beach towel is 36"x72" or larger. Anything smaller and you're back to fighting for coverage. The Salt Earth towel hits exactly 36"x72" — enough for full-body coverage without being unwieldy to pack. If you're tall (6'2"+), look for 40"x72" options, but expect to pay more.

Understand the Fabric — Turkish Cotton vs. Terry Cloth

Turkish cotton (also called peshtemal) is lightweight, quick-drying, and sand-resistant — ideal for beach use. Terry cloth is thicker and more absorbent but takes longer to dry and traps sand. For beach days, Turkish cotton wins. For post-shower luxury, terry cloth edges ahead. This pick is Turkish cotton, making it the smarter beach choice.
