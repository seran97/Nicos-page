Buying Guide

What to Look For in a Lipliner Shade

A short primer on lip liner formulas helps explain why some glide effortlessly and others drag or feather.

Check the formula textureCreamy formulas like soft-matte liners apply more smoothly on drier lips, while harder pencils can tug and require more prep, especially in cooler weather.
Match the shade to your use caseA shade meant for natural definition sits closer to your lip's own tone, while one meant for full-lip fill needs to match your preferred lipstick shade.
Consider longevity versus comfortLong-wear matte liners tend to feel drier on the lips over time, so weigh staying power against comfort if you wear lip products most of the day.
