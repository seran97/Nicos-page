Will this fit any car with a CD slot?
It's designed for standard CD slot dimensions found in most vehicles, though very old or nonstandard slot sizes should be checked against the product's specifications.
Does the phone actually stay in place while driving?
With 720 reviews at 4.2 stars, most buyers report a secure hold during normal driving, though very rough roads can occasionally test the magnetic grip's limits.
How fast does it charge compared to a regular MagSafe charger?

How does this compare to a vent-mounted magnetic holder?
