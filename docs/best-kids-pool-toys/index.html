Buying Guide

What to Look For in a Kids' Pool Toys

Small toddler water toys hinge on a few safety and durability details that aren't always obvious from photos.

Check piece size for choking hazardsAny toy with small floating pieces needs scrutiny if there are younger children in the house. Look for age recommendations and piece dimensions before buying.
Evaluate magnet durability claimsMagnetic strength can fade with repeated water exposure over a season. Reviews mentioning multi-month use are more reliable than first-week impressions for this.
Think about multi-surface useToys that work in a bathtub, kiddie pool, and water table interchangeably give more value per dollar than single-use pool toys.
